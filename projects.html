Tales of Graces en Español
Improved Hacking		 2018
Addendum /
Original work.		 Nintendo Wii JP-only game that got a Spanish fan-translation, the original release had problems,
misc text isn't translated, any text that uses textures requires Dolphin, and for unknown
reasons it got split between two single-layer copies of the game.

My changes: Manually inserting the translated textures into the game files so a real Wii
can play it. Repoint many item descriptions to translate them, re-encode every cutscene
at a lower rate to fit the entire game as a file on a FAT32 file system.
Sonic Team Mini Game Collection 2020
Original work.		 GameCube Complete translation of the app used to get the rare Tails, Knuckles, and Amy Chao.
It's patched to work with the US carts, but EU can be enabled with a code.
Localized PuyoPop and ChuChu Rocket are taken from Billy Hatcher.
Due to the complicated nature of patching this because of the ROMS, I've created
a native Wii app that replicates the "Rare Chao Transfer" portion of this app.
Instead of writing the Chao to a GBA, the data is written directly to a raw Memory Card.
Princess Ai/Out of Galaxy 2020
Original work.		 Nintendo Wii Full interface translation, the goal being not just to make it more accessible but to have
a base for comic book reading on Wii.
The NVCD format was reversed by me for the purpose of adding new comics.
The problem is that the reader copies the entire comic to RAM, and has a tight limit
of around 16 MB, this forced me to give up, and rework WiiXplorer into a better reader.
3DS GBA VC
Color Restoration		 2016
Original work.		 Nintendo 3DS When Nintendo released the 10 GBA ambassador games, they dimmed the colors
and applied a motion blur effect. I didn't like this, and looked at the footer data
to reverse it until I figured out how to get the normal brightness working, soon after
I found the value for adjusting the motion blur filter.

Since then I've greatly enjoyed using my 3DS as a GBA and it served as inspiration
for the various GBA save data tools I've made.
